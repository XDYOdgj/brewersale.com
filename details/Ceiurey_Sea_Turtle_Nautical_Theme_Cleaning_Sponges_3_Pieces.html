Eco-Friendly Nautical Theme Cleaning Sponges

Eco-friendly compostable kitchen sponges made from natural cellulose, featuring a unique nautical theme design with sea turtle, starfish, and shell patterns. Ideal for home cleaning and daily chores, these non-scratch sponges are gentle on delicate surfaces and tough on grime.

With a soft cotton top layer that ensures safe and effective cleaning, these sponges are perfect for scrubbing pots, wiping countertops, and cleaning bathroom tiles.
