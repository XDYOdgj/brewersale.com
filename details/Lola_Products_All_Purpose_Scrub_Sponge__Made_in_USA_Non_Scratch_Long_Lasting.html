Lola's Pot Brite All Purpose Scrub Sponge

Introducing the Lola's Pot Brite scrub sponge, a powerful and efficient cleaning tool designed to make your kitchen tasks easier and more enjoyable. Engineered with advanced Hydrocell Foam and ScrubClean technology, this sponge delivers a clean, odorless cleaning experience that's tough on grime but gentle on surfaces.

Key Features

  • Hydrocell Foam + ScrubClean technology for powerful, odorless cleaning
  • Non-scratch design safe for use on non-stick and coated cookware
  • Durable fibers that maintain strength and absorption over time
  • Wave surface design for deep cleaning in grooves and corners
  • Ultra-comfortable grip and reusable for eco-friendly cleaning

Why Choose This Sponge?

Whether you're washing dishes, mugs, or non-stick pans, the Lola's Pot Brite scrub sponge is your must-have kitchen companion. Its unique double-sided design includes an abrasive scourer side for tackling tough stains and a non-scratch side for preserving the delicate finish of your cookware. Plus, it's easy to rinse and dry, ensuring a hygienic and hassle-free cleaning routine every time.
